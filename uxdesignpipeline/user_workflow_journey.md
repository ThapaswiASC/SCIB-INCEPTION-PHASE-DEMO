# BridgeNow Finance Application Journey - User Workflow Documentation

## Project Overview
**Epic:** E2 — BridgeNow Finance Application Journey & Communication  
**Story ID:** VRVTEMP-390  
**Vision:** Deliver a seamless, fully digital application journey for BridgeNow Finance, with clear communication and distinct branding across web, app, and ads.

## Experience Framework

### User Experience: Financial Product Discovery & Application
The BridgeNow Finance user experience encompasses multiple touchpoints from initial awareness to loan disbursement. Key experiences include:
- Product Discovery
- Application Initiation
- Eligibility Assessment
- Application Processing
- Status Tracking
- Communication Management

---

## Scenario 1: New Customer Product Discovery & Application

### Context
**User:** Sarah, a 32-year-old marketing professional, needs a personal loan to consolidate her credit card debt. She has heard about BridgeNow Finance through digital advertising and wants to explore the product offering and apply if eligible.

**Situation:** Sarah is actively researching financial products online and comparing different lenders. She values transparency, quick processing, and competitive rates.

### User Goal
To discover BridgeNow Finance product details, understand eligibility requirements, and complete a loan application efficiently with clear communication throughout the process.

### Business Goal
To convert qualified prospects into loan applicants through clear product positioning, streamlined application flow, and automated processing while maintaining regulatory compliance and brand differentiation.
