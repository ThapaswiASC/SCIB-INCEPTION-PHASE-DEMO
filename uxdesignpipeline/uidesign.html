Improvement Recommendations
Follow these steps to improve document quality
🔍
Fix Blur Issues
Hold your device steady and ensure the document is in focus before capturing. Tap the screen to focus on the document.
💡
Reduce Glare
Move to a location with even lighting. Avoid direct light sources and reflective surfaces. Tilt the document slightly if needed.
📐
Include All Edges
Make sure the entire document fits within the camera frame. Leave a small margin around all edges.
