                <li><strong>Task Management:</strong> The core workflow involves creating, viewing, editing, and moving tasks within the Kanban board with real-time synchronization.</li>

                <li><strong>Accessibility Features:</strong> Dedicated paths for keyboard navigation help and screen reader optimized views ensure inclusive design.</li>
