            <p><strong>Main User Paths:</strong></p>
            <ul>

                <li><strong>Navigation:</strong> Switching between different board views and accessing settings</li>
                <li><strong>Real-time Updates:</strong> Receiving and processing live updates from other users</li>
                <li><strong>Error Handling:</strong> Managing system errors and providing recovery options</li>

            <p><strong>Key Interaction Points:</strong></p>

                <li>Header settings and user menu</li>
